About LMCMUN

La Martiniere Calcutta Model United Nations (LMCMUN) has the privilege of being the pioneer in the field of MUN Conferences in Eastern India. Hosted by the students of La Martiniere for Boys and La Martiniere for Girls Schools, Kolkata, LMCMUN’s journey began in 2009 in the form of a conference with 30 schools. Over the years it has evolved to the level of an international conference with LMCMUN 2012, LMCMUN 2013 hosting schools from beyond the borders, thereby making it the first international MUN conference in Eastern India. With 7 innovative and challenging committees, LMCMUN 2014 promises to be an enriching experience for all participating schools and a satisfying journey for those involved.


About

A Model UN conference is a simulation of the proceedings that take place at the headquarters of the United Nations Organization in New York, USA. At such conferences, students receive a first-hand experience of the working of various committees in the UN. Students are presented with complex problems which are being discussed and deliberated in the UN. The students, posing as delegates representing various countries, approach the situation keeping in mind their country’s foreign policy and their current stand in the international forum. Thus, a Model UN Conference not only hones a student’s talents in public speaking but also edifies the skills of compromise, diplomacy and negotiation. It helps students develop a healthy interest in world politics and become the leaders of tomorrow.
